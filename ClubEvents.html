General Body Meetings

General body meetings take place biweekly on Fridays at 2pm with some adjustments for holidays and breaks (Fall 2023 semester). Our first meeting is on September 15th at 2pm in Rockefeller.

Each meeting will contain a presentation or lecture from either a guest speaker, a professor, or a member of the club on a topic in Quantum Computing. Pizza will also be provided at each meeting.

All General Body Meeting dates for Fall 2023:

  • September 15th
  • September 29th
  • October 13th
  • October 27th
  • November 3rd
  • November 17th
  • Decemeber 1st

Fall Fest

Fall Fest will take place from November 6th to November 12th. The focuse of Fall Fest is to welcome students of any major into the club and introduce them to quantum computing.
